Let's get started!

Upon clicking "start," you will be given a series of tasks to complete on Taobao, until 15 minutes is reached. The study will last for approximately 15 minutes regardless of task completion speed. Please perform the tasks as closely as you would in real life.On completion of the study, this extension will be automatically uninstalled and you will receive a ¥10 in Tmall points in the mailbox of the email you have provided.

Please note that all currently open Chrome browser windows will close when the study begins.
