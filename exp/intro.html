Let's get started!

Upon clicking "start," you will be given a series of shopping task simulations until 8 minutes is reached or 4 tasks are completed, whatever is longer. Please perform the tasks as closely as you would in real life; the study will take approximately the same time, no matter your speed. On completion of the study, this extension will be automatically uninstalled and you will receive a ¥10 in Tmall points in the mailbox of the email you have provided.

During the course of the study, you must stay on this window. Closing the window will result in ending the study.
